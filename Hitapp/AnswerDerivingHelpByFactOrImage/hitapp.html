Evaluating if the image/fact helps to derive the answer of the question


Instructions
Instructions

If someone asked for the question , would the image/fact be helpful to derive answer?

Definition of relevance:

  • 1. Does the image/fact help in deriving the answer completely
    I.e. Answer fully explained by the fact
  • 2. Does the image/fact help in deriving the answer partially
    I.e. Some parts of the answer explained by the fact
  • 3. Is the image/fact related but not needed to derive the answer
  • 4. Image/fact is completely unrelated
