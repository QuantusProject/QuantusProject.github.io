May 20th update:

Hi!

We got some feedback from some workers and here are some clearfication:

  1. "The facts provided are unrelated in terms of Topic/Key words/Entity" in the "Tell us about the facts" section is used to mark if the facts had strong relationship with each other. The Example 5 is an example about this case.
  2. The answer should be present in the fact completely and should not need the blue link. The blue link is only used to let you understand the topic.
  3. You can use the textbox in "Tell us about this question" to note any problem in the question regardless your answer of the "Can you understand what is being asked without reading the facts?".

May 17th update:

In this HitApp, you will be asked to judge the quality of some multi-reasoning Question-and-Answer(QnA) pairs.

A multi-reasoning QnA pair is one where the answer to the question is derived from information from several distinct facts/text snippets. We need your help in this validation app to gather great quality examples. Please reach out to us if you have feedback or any questions and we can iterate together!
