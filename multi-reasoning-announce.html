Some important tips/suggestions:

1. Information from the link provided can be used in the question only if the fact has that information fully contained. If information is missing then please add fact with full information and select that fact in your annotation.

2. You can now copy-paste easily with fact highlight feature - just highlight the piece of text and you will see a copy icon on left of highlighted text to quickly copy and lookup and do further research if needed.

3. Answer in fully formed sentences are preferable.
4. Make sure your questions are not just Yes/No variations or Comparison type of questions.
5. Also, when you select facts make sure the answer for the question requires distinct information from ALL of your selected facts to derive the answer. If answer is completely contained in one fact then the judgment does not qualify as valid.
